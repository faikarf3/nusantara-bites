Authentic Indonesian Flavors

At Nusantara Bites, we bring the rich culinary heritage of Indonesia to New York City. Our dishes are prepared using traditional recipes and authentic ingredients, offering you a true taste of Indonesia's diverse cuisine.

From the spicy flavors of Padang to the sweet notes of Javanese cuisine, our menu represents the best of Indonesian cooking.
