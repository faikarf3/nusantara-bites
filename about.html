Our Story

A journey through Indonesian culinary heritage

The Nusantara Bites Story

Founded by Chef Budi Santoso, Nusantara Bites brings the rich culinary heritage of Indonesia to the heart of New York City. Our name "Nusantara" refers to the Indonesian archipelago, representing our commitment to showcasing the diverse flavors from across Indonesia's 17,000 islands.

What started as a small family restaurant has grown into a beloved destination for authentic Indonesian cuisine, while maintaining the warm hospitality and attention to detail that made us special from the beginning.

Our commitment to authenticity has earned us recognition from food critics and the Indonesian community alike. But our greatest achievement is sharing the vibrant flavors and rich culture of Indonesia with our guests.
